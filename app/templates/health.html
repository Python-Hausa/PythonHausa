COVID-19 Health & Safety

Your health is our priority. Please follow these safety measures during all in-person events.

General Guidelines

  • Wear a face mask at all times during in-person events.
  • Use provided hand sanitizers regularly.
  • Maintain physical distancing whenever possible.
  • Do not attend events if you're experiencing flu-like symptoms.

Event Safety Measures

  • All venues will have adequate ventilation and spacing.
  • Temperature checks at entry points.
  • First aid and safety support available on-site.
  • Hybrid/online participation options available for major sessions.

Let's Protect Each Other

We are committed to keeping our community safe. Let's work together by being responsible, caring, and informed.
