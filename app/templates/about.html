About Python Hausa Initiative

Building a stronger Northern Nigeria through Python programming — in our own language, Hausa.

Our Mission

To promote digital inclusion by teaching Python programming in the Hausa language, empowering youth and underserved communities across Northern Nigeria.

Our Vision

A future where every young person in Northern Nigeria has the skills, tools, and confidence to code, build, and contribute — in their mother tongue.

Our Core Objectives

Hausa Tutorials

Create Python guides and materials in Hausa to boost accessibility.

Community Events

Host meetups, bootcamps, and local workshops in different states.

Diversity & Inclusion

Empower women, youth, and underserved communities in tech.

Open Source

Promote Hausa participation in the global open-source ecosystem.

Why Teach in Hausa?

Many learners are left behind because most programming resources are in English. By providing resources in Hausa, we remove this barrier and make Python more inclusive and culturally relevant. This initiative is about unlocking potential — no matter your background or language.
