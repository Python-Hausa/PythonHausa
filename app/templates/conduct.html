Community Code of Conduct

Creating a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

Our Values

Python Hausa Initiative is dedicated to providing a safe, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of gender, age, disability, ethnicity, religion, or experience level.

Expected Behavior

  • Be respectful and kind in speech and actions.
  • Support each other — especially beginners.
  • Respect differing opinions and cultures.
  • Give constructive feedback with empathy.

Unacceptable Behavior

  • Harassment of any kind, including offensive comments or jokes.
  • Discrimination, intimidation, or threats.
  • Inappropriate or sexualized language or imagery.
  • Disruptive behavior in events or online spaces.

Reporting

If you witness or experience any violation of this Code of Conduct, please report it immediately to an organizer, team member, or via our contact page. Reports will be handled confidentially.
